Soundnode App is a Open Source project to support Soundcloud for Desktop platform.

Follow us on twitter http://www.twitter.com/soundnodeapp or for more information https://github.com/Soundnode/soundnode-app/wiki/Help

Be aware:
  • - Every Soundcloud third party has a limit of 15k streamable tracks.
  • - Not every track is streamable for copyrights reason (we automatically hide those tracks)
  • - We have no plans to implement/add/integrate another API e.g LastFM
